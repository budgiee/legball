Staff

Mika

Tournament Leader/Host

Mika, known as "mikasmeatball" on Twitch, is an Australian streamer renowned for her engaging Dead by Daylight (DbD) content. With over 2,000 hours of gameplay on console and more than 1,000 hours on PC. Her main characters include P100 Nea Karlsson and P100 Chucky/Tiffany.

Beyond streaming, Mika actively participates in the DbD community by organizing tournaments, offering players a platform to compete and connect. Her streams are characterized by a blend of competitive gameplay and a welcoming atmosphere, making her channel a hub for both seasoned players and newcomers.

For those interested in competitive play or seeking an engaging DbD community, Mika's channel offers a blend of high-level gameplay and community-driven events.

Benny

Tournament Co-Leader/Co-Host

Benny, also known as "legendgaming1023" is a dedicated Dead by Daylight (DbD) player, renowned for his mastery of the Hillbilly, achievingz P100 status with this high-skill killer. His deep understanding of the game’s mechanics and strategic gameplay make him a formidable presence in the fog.

Often seen alongside Mika, LegendGaming1023 brings a dynamic synergy to their matches, whether they’re surviving together or dominating in the 2v8 mode.

Passionate about every aspect of DbD, LegendGaming1023 isn’t just a co-host; he’s a core member of the community, helping shape tournaments and events with his gameplay experience and positive energy.
